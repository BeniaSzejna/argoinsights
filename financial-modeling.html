What we do

Models built for real decisions

Most businesses start with a spreadsheet thrown together in a hurry. It works until it doesn't — until an investor asks a question you can't answer, or a hiring decision relies on cash flow projections you're no longer sure you trust.

We build financial models that are clear, flexible, and built around how your business actually works. Whether you need a three-year forecast for a fundraise, a rolling budget you can update monthly, or a scenario tool to stress-test a big decision — we'll build it with you.

We also make sure you can use it. Every model we deliver comes with a walkthrough so you're never dependent on us to answer a basic question.

What's typically included

  • 3–5 year revenue and cost forecasts
  • Monthly cash flow and runway projections
  • Scenario modelling (base, upside, downside)
  • Headcount and hiring plan integration
  • Unit economics — CAC, LTV, payback period
  • Investor-ready P&L, balance sheet & cash flow
  • KPI dashboard with key drivers clearly labelled
  • Sensitivity analysis on your key assumptions

Who it's for

04 types

Founders raising investment A model that stands up to investor scrutiny — with clear assumptions, credible projections, and the flexibility to answer follow-up questions in the room.
Growing businesses planning ahead Moving from reactive to proactive — a proper budget and rolling forecast so you can plan hiring, investment, and expansion without flying blind.
Businesses approaching an exit or sale A clean, well-documented model that demonstrates value to acquirers and supports a credible narrative through due diligence.
Teams evaluating a specific decision Launching a new product, entering a new market, or making a large hire? We'll model the scenarios so you can weigh the numbers before you commit.

How we work

04 steps

01 Discovery call

We start by understanding your business, your goal for the model, and the decisions it needs to support. No jargon, no templates pushed at you before we've listened.

02 Build & iterate

We build the model in close collaboration — sharing drafts early, incorporating your knowledge of the business, and refining until every number reflects how you actually operate.

03 Review & walkthrough

We walk you through the finished model in detail — explaining the logic, the assumptions, and the levers you can pull. You leave knowing how to use it, not just what it says.

04 Ongoing support

Business changes fast. We're available for updates, refreshes, and new scenarios as your situation evolves — no need to start from scratch every time something shifts.
